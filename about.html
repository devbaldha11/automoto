OUR JOURNEY

The foundations of Automoto as an Autonomous driving company were established in 2013 at Rajkot, Gujarat by our founder. In the nearly a decades since its inception, Automoto continuously grew and evolved, transitioning from a pure manufacturing company to a multi millions conglomerate operating in businesses.

Through this journey, Automoto's focus on outstanding quality has been consistent and unrelenting. Be it about setting up India's first autonomous driverless car or introducing path-breaking technologies like groundbreaking perception-enabled sensor technology in India.

In its entrepreneurial journey, Automoto focused on building and leveraging relationships with the best in the world, in its relentless pursuit of excellence. Starting off as a manufacturer of as an AI company, evolved Automoto by partnering with many global leaders.

OUR MISSION

Redefining the Way We Move

Automoto's vision is to "create the most compelling car company of the 21st century by driving the world's transition to autonomous vehicles", while its mission is "to accelerate the advent of sustainable transport by bringing compelling mass-market of cars to market as soon as possible". Automoto used a transitional business model as its ecosystem grows. Automoto building its most affordable car yet, Automoto continues to make products accessible and affordable to more and more people, ultimately accelerating the advent of clean transport and clean energy production.

To achieve our goal of having the safest factories in the world, Automoto is taking a proactive approach to safety, requiring production employees to participate in a multi-day training program before ever setting foot on the factory floor. From there, Automoto continues to provide on-the-job training and track performance daily so that improvements can be made quickly. The result is that Automoto's safety rate continues to improve while production ramps.
